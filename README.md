**AtliQ Hardware Company Introduction:**

Atliq Hardware is a global hardware and electronics company specializing in the sale and manufacturing of personal computers, laptops, printers, mice, and various peripheral devices. The company offers a diverse range of technology products designed to meet the needs of both individual consumers and businesses across international markets.
Some of its major customers and distribution partners include Croma, Flipkart, Best Buy, Staples, and Amazon. These platforms help AtliQ deliver its products to end consumers across multiple countries.

**Problem Statement:**

The objective of this project is to develop an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard for Atliq Hardware to analyze sales and financial performance. The dashboard helps identify key business insights such as top-selling products, profitable customers, sales trends, profit margins, and financial stability, enabling better strategic and data-driven decision-making.

**Sales Analysis:**

**Objectives**

1.	Top 10 Products with Sales Increase: Identify which products had the biggest increase in sales from 2020 to 2021.
2.	Division Sales Report: Create a report showing total revenue in 2020 and 2021 and how much sales grew.
3.	Top and Bottom 5 Sold Products: Identify the top 5 and bottom 5 products sold by quantity.
4.	New Products in 2021: List new products Atliq started selling in 2021.
5.	Top 5 Sales Countries in 2021: Identify the five countries with the highest sales in 2021.
6.	Customer Net Sales Performance Report: Overview of revenue generated from customers.
7.	Market Performance vs. Sales Targets Comparison: Analysis of sales efforts against goals and market conditions.
